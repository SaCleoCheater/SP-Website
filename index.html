What do we host?

Here at SP, we host currently a TTT server, however we plan to implement more servers in the future. We're already considering a CS:GO server along with a possible TF2 server, so there's much variety for the user. We're also using Linux powered servers now, and they run very well.

What are we doing here?

We aim to provide fast and enjoyable experiences of Garry's Mod servers to fellow players, and Members. We're continuing a legendary community with a legendary server of which has been inactive for almost a year; Since the Garry's Mod 13 update.

Who are we?

We're here because, we've volunteered to continue a community known as Stoned Potatoes, SP. The previous SP had unfortunately announced it's closure of gameservers in mid-2013, however there has not been a TTT or Fretta server online in a long while. We aim to change that and restore the old SP.

So join our servers!

We can't wait to see you online. A friendly, welcoming community is waiting for you!
